My name is Wenqing Yang (杨文清). I majored in pure mathematics when I was an undergraduate student. Measure Theory, Real Analysis and Probability always inspires me to keep learning and exploring.

It is for this reason that I later turned to applied probability theory, so I majored in Statistics when I was a graduate student. In the process of learning statistics, what excites me the most is solving problems, by building parametric and nonparametric models, and getting the optimal solution to a practical problem. I have been studying traditional statistics methods since September 2018.

But there are still some topics and real world problems cannot be solved by definite models. So I began to learn nonparametric methods, like Machine Learning and Deep Learning from 2018, trying to use computrer science methods to solving mathematical and Statistical problems.

On the other way, I'm also a talented Machine Learning Engineer and Data Scientist with 6+ years of experience. I learned basic computer science theory for more than 5 years and very interested in developing software using Java and Ruby.
